# Webstackbuilders Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are dedicated to providing a welcoming, safe, and professional environment for all contributors, partners, and community members, regardless of background, gender, sexual orientation, disability, race, religion, or technology choices. We subscribe to a rigorous standard of open-source and professional conduct, mirroring leading industry practices established within the cloud-native ecosystem.

All participants in Webstackbuilders environments—including public repositories, issue trackers, and direct communication channels—are expected to behave professionally, treat others with respect, and maintain a high level of collaborative integrity.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behaviors include, but are not limited to:

- Harassment, intimidation, or stalking.
- Offensive, derogatory, or exclusionary verbal or written comments.
- Public or private harassment, including unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
- Sustained disruption of electronic discussions, meetings, or other collaborative threads.
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission.

## Reporting an Incident

If you experience, observe, or witness unacceptable behavior—or have any other structural safety concerns—please report it immediately to our operations review team at **conduct@webstackbuilders.com**.

To assist us in conducting a thorough and rapid review, please include as many of the following details as possible in your initial communication:

- **Your contact information**: Full name and your preferred channel of follow-up communication.
- **Involved parties**: Names, handles, or pseudonyms of any individuals directly involved, along with any relevant witnesses.
- **Account of occurrences**: A clear description of what occurred, indicating whether you believe the incident is isolated or ongoing.
- **Supporting evidence**: Links to publicly available records (such as repository issues, pull requests, or communication archives), screenshots, or logs where applicable.
- **Context**: Any additional context or history that will assist our team in assessing the situation fairly.

## Enforcement and Discretion

All submitted reports will be handled with strict discretion and evaluated with the utmost seriousness. Upon receipt of a report, the following structural protocols will be enforced:

1. **Acknowledgment**: A representative from Webstackbuilders will acknowledge receipt of your report within **24 hours**.
2. **Review Process**: Our response team will review the incident log contextually, gather necessary background factors, and contact you securely if any clarifying questions arise.
3. **Conflict of Interest Safeguards**: If a member of the response or operations team is named or involved in the report, they will be immediately recused from all review proceedings, triage tasks, and decision-making actions regarding that case.
4. **Confidentiality**: We will honor all valid confidentiality requests to ensure the absolute protection and safety of victims of abuse.
5. **Resolution**: Webstackbuilders reserves the right to take appropriate corrective action against any participant found violating this policy, ranging from formal warnings to permanent bans from our workspaces and repositories.
